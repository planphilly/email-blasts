Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Monday 4/18
10:00 am: City Council’s committee on Licenses & Inspections considers regulations on public notice for demolitions and requirements for fire-resistant materials. Free.
5:30 pm: Tyler Arts’s monthly Crafts & Drafts happy hour for creative professionals. Free.
6:00 pm: Help plan this year’s events for Benson Park in South Kensington. Free.
Tuesday 4/19
8:00 am: Urban Land Institute’s Spring Meeting is in Philadelphia for the first time in 20 years, with sessions all day Tuesday through Thursday. Here’s the schedule, if you’re planning to attend.
6:00 pm: Weigh in on the future of Roosevelt Blvd at the third public forum at the Lenfest Center. Free.
6:30 pm: The Take Your Time Ride series is back, with a beginner’s bike ride from Rittenhouse Square to Fishtown. Free.
Wednesday 4/20
6:00 pm: The fourth public forum on Roosevelt Blvd at the Radisson Hotel in Northeast Philly. Free.
6:00 pm: Celebration of Center City Living fundraiser hosted by Center City Residents Association Ticket prices vary.
Thursday 4/21
4:30 pm: The fifth and final public meeting on Roosevelt Blvd. Free.
6:00 pm: The Urban Geek Drinks monthly happy hour at Frankford Hall. Free.
6:30 pm: The first public meeting on the Upper North District Plan. Free.
7:00 pm: A state representative candidate forum for the 182nd and 175th districts, hosted by Southeast Philly civics. Free.
Friday 4/22
5:00 pm: Opening Reception for "Mixing Memories - Sharing History" at Cliveden. Free.
6:00 pm: Philadelphia Magic Gardens kicks off their Twilight in the Gardens seasonal live music series. BYOB. $12-18.
Saturday 4/23
11:00 am: Zipline down Broad Street, or not, at PIFA’s 2016 Finale Street Fair. Free.
