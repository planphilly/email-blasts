Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Monday 6/6
1:00 pm: City Council’s Committee on Licenses and Inspections holds a public hearing on a bill holding businesses accountable for nuisance behavior in the vicinity of their stores, among other topics. Free.
Tuesday 6/7
2:00 pm: Council’s Streets committee hears a proposal from Councilman Bobby Henon to add a buffered bike lane on Tyson Avenue from Roosevelt Boulevard to Frankford Ave. Free.
5:30 pm: Here’s My Chance turns five, and throws a party at Stratus Lounge. Pay what you will.
6:00 pm: Show off your knowledge of Southwest Center City at SOSNA’s neighborhood-themed Quizzo night. Free.
7:00 pm: 'Making Good Neighbors – The Meaning of Integration in Post War Mt. Airy' is the topic of author Abigail Perkiss’s presentation at WMAN’s annual meeting. Free.
7:00 pm: Fishtown zoners hear proposals for a garage-fronted home, and outdoor seating near Thompson and Front. Free.
Wednesday 6/8
9:00 am: The Bicycle Coalition is seeking supporters for a trip to Harrisburg to lobby state lawmakers for three safer streets bills. Free.
6:00 pm: East Passyunk neighbors host a fundraiser at the P.O.P.E. for the South Philadelphia Older Adult Center garden. $25.
6:00 pm: Center for Emerging Visual Artists hosts their annual Art Party fundraiser. $40.
Thursday 6/9
3:30 pm: Land Bank board meeting. Free.
6:00 pm: Young Involved Philadelphia hosts a panel on poverty and inequality with WHYY’s Katie Colaneri, Councilwoman Helen Gym, Dwayne Wharton of the Food Trust, and Cynthia Figueroa of Congreso. Free.
6:00 pm: The Philly Block Project hosts an opening for the Archive Collective’s ‘South Kensington 19122’ exhibition. Free.
Saturday 6/11
10:00 am: East Park Reservoir tour for Fairmount Park Conservancy members. $35 memberships, free for members.
10:00 am: Plein Air outdoor arts competition in Chestnut Hill. Free.
1:00 pm: Ben Leech guides a BIKESPLORATION Tour of mid-century Philly architecture, hosted by Hidden City and SPOKE Mag $15 general, $10 Hidden City members and students.
