Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Before we get to our regularly-scheduled programming, we want to tell you about two upcoming events that PlanPhilly is hosting:
Next Sunday, 10/11 at 1pm, we’re hosting a DesignPhiladelphia event called Mode Shift, looking at Philly’s changing land use and transportation politics. We’ll look back at the Nutter administration’s record, and debrief on what we learned reporting on the 2016 campaign with ex-MOTU head Rina Cutler, ZBA appointee Greg Pastore, PWD’s Director of Strategic Partnerships, Chrisine Knapp, and PlanPhilly editor Ashley Hahn.
Then on Wednesday 10/21, we’re hosting Neighborhood Civics 101 during this year’s State of Young Philly week. PlanPhilly reporter Jared Brey will moderate a conversation with members of some of Philly’s best-organized neighborhood civic groups about what civics do, what powers they have, and why Young Involved Philadelphians (and any of our readers!) should get involved with them. There is a $10 cover for this one.
