Wednesday 8/12
8:00 am: A Women Bike PHL Coffee Club meet-up at Peddler Coffee. Free.
5:30 pm: Community Design Collaborative presents a screening of The Land, about a Welsh adventure playground, with director Erin Davis at Center for Architecture. $5 - $10.
6:00 pm: Artist Nile Livingston hosts a paint party on the patio at the Dahlak Paradise Ethiopian Restaurant in West Philly. $30.
6:00 pm: An architectural walking tour of Rittenhouse Square West with the Preservation Alliance. $10 - $15.
