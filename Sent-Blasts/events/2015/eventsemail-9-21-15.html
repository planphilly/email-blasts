Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Monday 9/21
10:00 am: City Council’s Appropriations Committee considers amendments to the 2016 capital budget. Free.
6:30 pm: East Kensington Neighbors Association general meeting at Philadelphia Brewing Co. Free.
7:00 pm: Learn about the Clean Your 16 campaign and other neighborhood cleaning and beautification strategies at Bella Vista Neighbors Association’s general meeting. Free.
Tuesday 9/22
9:00 am: The Historical Commission’s Architecture Committee meets in Room 578 at City Hall. Free.
6:30 pm: Get the latest news on I-95 Revive and Kensington Community Food Co-op at ORCA’s general meeting. Free.
Wednesday 9/23
5:30 pm: West Philly artist Nile Livingston hosts another Philly Paint Party at Dahlak Paradis. $30, materials included. + food and drink specials.
6:30 pm: A lecture from University of Notre Dame professor Duncan Stroik on how the Catholic Church can reclaim a role as patroness of sacred arts and architecture. $25 - $35.
6:30 pm: South Kensington Community Partners’ monthly zoning meeting. Free.
Thursday 9/24
6:00 pm: Learn about the redevelopment of the Burlington, VT waterfront from Main Street Landing CEO Melinda Moulton at PennDesign. Free.
Friday 9/25
1:00 pm: Learn to design a garden plot like a pro in a three-part class at the Barnes Arboretum. Registration required.
Saturday 9/26
10:00 am: Take advantage of Philly’s almost-Open Streets weekend with the Pope Ride -- a relaxed pace group bike ride through the almost car-free security perimeter. Free.
