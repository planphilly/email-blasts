Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Tuesday 11/3
7:00 am - 8:00 pm: Election Day! Find your polling place here.
7:00 am: A morning bike tour of all the Next Stop: Democracy polling place art installations. Free.
Wednesday 11/4
3:00 pm: Jeffrey Sachs of Earth Institute and Columbia keynotes a Penn IUR and Perry World House program on sustainable global urbanization. Free, registration required.
4:00 pm: A conversation about cutting Philly’s GHG emissions by 80% by 2050, with Katherine Gajewski of the Mayor’s Office of Sustainability and researchers from Drexel. Free.
5:30 pm: Community Design Collaborative and DVAEYC host play scholar Dr. Anna Beresin, author of The Art of Play, at Center for Architecture. $5 - $10.
7:30 pm: Learn about the mystery of the Kensington Rune Stone at Philly Nerd Nite. $5.
Thursday 11/5
8:00 am: Design Advocacy Group hosts a roundtable discussion on reimagining Market East, with Paul Levy and reps from Brickstone, NREA, and RBA. Free.
Friday 11/6
5:00 pm: Mural Arts has world-renowned artist Richard Haas and muralist Jon Laidacker. Free.
Saturday 11/7
9:30 am: NKCDC needs volunteers to clean up the corridor between Front and Norris. Free.
11:00 am: Join Hidden City and SPOKE Mag for a “bikesploration” tour of the new Manayunk Bridge Trail. $10 - $15.
Sunday 11/8
11:00 am: Explore some of the city's finest grave architecture on a hike through Mt. Moriah Cemetery with Hidden City. $15 - $20.
