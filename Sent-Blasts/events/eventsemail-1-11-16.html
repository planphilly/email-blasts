Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Monday 1/11
4:00 pm: The Federal Railroad Administration’s local meeting reviewing the various proposals for a higher-speed Northeast Corridor. Free.
7:30 pm: Learn about how the Workforce Housing Initiative will work in Francisville at the Francisville Information Network’s monthly meeting. Free.
Wednesday 1/13
9:30 am: A crash course for architecture and design professionals on working with state and regional public sector agencies, hosted by SMPS. $55-75.
5:30 pm: The Planning Commission hosts their fourth annual celebration at WHYY, celebrating progress on the Philadelphia2035 plan. Free, RSVP required.
5:30 pm: Join the Community Design Collaborative and psychologist and folklorist Anna Beresin for a workshop inspired by her book, The Art of Play. $5-10.
6:00 pm: A meet-and-greet with new City Councilmembers Helen Gym, Derek Green, and Allan Domb in Chestnut Hill. Free.
6:00 pm: Learn about volunteer opportunities with the Bicycle Coalition at their volunteer night. Free.
Thursday 1/14
8:00 am: At Design Advocacy Group’s monthly meeting, Fon Wang’s PennDesign students will present a conservation plan for Sharswood “for the purpose of guiding development while retaining historic, cultural, and living resources.” Free.
5:30 pm: Flying Kite Media’s fifth birthday celebration at the Trestle Inn. Free.
6:00 pm: WHYY hosts the premiere of Mercy Street, PBS’s first American-made drama in more than a decade, about the lives of Americans on the home front during the Civil War. Free.
7:00 pm: The monthly South Jersey Urbanists happy hour meet-up at Iron Hill Brewery. Free.
Saturday 1/16
10:30 am: A tour of the Rail Park, Phase 1 from Friends of the Rail Park and Center City District. Free.
