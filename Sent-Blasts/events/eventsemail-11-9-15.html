Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Monday 11/9
6:00 pm: A lecture on what makes places great by Michael Stern, principal at Strada. Stern worked on the Pittsburgh Downtown Plan and the Pittsburgh Regional Parks Master Plan before moving to Philly. Free.
6:00 pm: A public meeting on the Lower Southwest District Plan at Ezekiel Baptist Church. Free.
6:30 pm: PlanPhilly’s Jon Geeting is on a panel at Field House about building positive relationships between new arrivals and long-standing community members in changing neighborhoods. $5.
Tuesday 11/10
2:00 pm: Tax lien sale changes are up for discussion in City Council’s Finance committee. Free.
6:00 pm: Neighborhood Time Exchange hosts a discussion on placemaking, questioning “the making of place, change and spaces in the city.” Free.
6:00 pm: A public meeting about extending the Pennypack Trail. Free.
7:00 pm: Weigh in on I-95 noise barrier designs at a public meeting hosted by PennDOT. Free.
Wednesday 11/11
7:00 pm: WHYY, PlanPhilly and the Historical Society of Pennsylvania (HSP) present a screening of the PBS documentary series Visionaries and a discussion moderated by PlanPhilly editor Ashley Hahn. Free, registration required.
Thursday 11/12
10:00 am: DVRPC’s Healthy Communities Task Force has presentations on Building Better Bike Share, Safe Routes to School, and a discussion of complete streets and equity. Free.
10:00 am: City Council is taking up the Gallery Neighborhood Improvement District, Mayfair BID, a bunch of land transfers to the PRA, and a couple dozen other agenda items. Free.
5:00 pm: Opening Night of the Franklin Square Holiday Festival. Free.
5:00 pm: Project HOME is previewing a plan for 30 units in a four story building on the Bucks hosiery factory fire site. Free.
6:00 pm: Central Delaware Advocacy Group holds their November board meeting. Free.
Friday 11/13
9:00 am: Playgrounds for Useful Knowledge is a day-long “action-research project” in South Philly between Cohabitation Strategies and Mural Arts. Subject matter looks fascinating but strongly resists summary. $15.
5:00 pm: A public reception and celebration of the year’s artistic creations at Neighborhood Time Exchange. Free.
Saturday 11/14
9:00 am: Love Your Park Day! Find a local park clean-up in your area or join these clean-ups in Tacony and Fairmount that readers advertised on our Events page. Free.
