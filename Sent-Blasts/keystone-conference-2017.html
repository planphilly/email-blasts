May 10: Join us for
Urban Ideas Worth Stealing
What are tactics that have worked for Philly that could be replicated throughout the keystone state? What are best practices that other cities in Pennsylvania have executed? Join our friends at Keystone Crossroads for a one-day conference on 'Urban Ideas Worth Stealing.'

The PlanPhilly team will moderate panels on the pillars of urbanism:


Jim Saksa moderates the panel on how transportation planners have creatively used funds and projects to heal the scars of during urban renewal.

Jake Blumgart leads a panel of experts and practitioners to explore integration and benefits of immigrant entrepreneurship as an economic engine for traditional Main Streets and downtowns.

Diana Lu will discuss with practitioners and business owners the programs, policies, and realities of addressing food deserts in low-income communities.
