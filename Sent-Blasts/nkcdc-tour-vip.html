What’s new for New Kensington CDC? On Friday, April 28th, PlanPhilly’s getting an on-the-ground tour of NKCDC’s newest projects with the representatives from NKCDC as our guides.

We’ll start at the the nearly-finished Orinoka Civic House - an affordable mixed-use development that will also be NKCDC’s new headquarters - and walk down Kensington Avenue to visit Porch Light’s new Kensington Storefront where NKCDC executive director Felix Torres-Colon will share his visions for the organization.
