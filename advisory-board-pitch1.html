Dear Advisory Board Members,

We hope you had a restful and happy Thanksgiving. As I reflected on this year, I’m enormously grateful for all of the good things happening at PlanPhilly.

With that in mind, we want you to be the first to know that PlanPhilly is embarking on an end-of-year campaign on Tuesday. We are aiming to raise $20,000 from 400 supporters during the month of December.

We are hoping to see 100% participation from our advisory board – it’s a strong demonstration of your belief in our work. We ask that you consider a contribution that’s meaningful to you.

There are other ways to help too.

  • Can you help us spread the word about our work and this campaign? Share this link with your friends and colleagues, letting them know why you support PlanPhilly:

    http://planphilly.nationbuilder.com/donate?recruiter_id={{ recipient.id }}
  • We will also be doing a matching challenge – where gifts from our readers will be matched up to a certain dollar amount – during the week of December 14. We’ve received some generous pledges for that challenge, including from one advisory board member. If you’re interested or able to put your contribution toward that challenge, please call or email me. (215-429-1278 or ahahn@planphilly.com)

All contributions to this campaign are tax-deductible and will be entirely dedicated to funding PlanPhilly’s journalism in our 10th year.

I am incredibly proud of the work PlanPhilly does, and our longstanding commitment to delivering strong journalism about design, development, mobility, and planning. Our role has renewed importance as the city looks toward a new mayor, new council, a continuing development boom, and a contracting local media environment.

Thank you for joining us and for your support -
