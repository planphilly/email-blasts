Each week the PlanPhilly staff curates our top picks of the week’s most informative, entertaining, and important events from our events page . Hosting an event? Submit it here and we’ll consider including it in our weekly email.
Monday 6/27
2:00 pm: The newly restored “Our Flag Unfurled” mural is rededicated, with music and food provided. Free.
6:30 pm: Northern Liberties Neighbors Association June zoning meeting. Agenda includes a proposal for a brewery at 6th and Spring Garden. Free.
Tuesday 6/28
8:30 am: “Build A School Garden,” presented by the Philadelphia Horticultural Society, kicks off its three-day course on how to design, build, and maintain a school garden. $50.
4:00 pm: ULI Philadelphia Presents: Brewing Success: How Breweries Are Transforming Real Estate at the Philadelphia Brewing Company. Prices vary, registration required.
Wednesday 6/29
5:00 pm: Parks on Tap launches at the Schuylkill Banks. As much as you want to spend on craft beer.
6:30 pm: South Kensington Community Partners Zoning Meeting. Free.
Thursday 6/30
6:00 pm: SPOKE Magazine hosts a launch party for their latest issue, and a conversation with the issue’s writers, including PlanPhilly’s Jim Saksa. $10.
8:30 pm: ‘National Treasure’ screening at Penn’s Landing as a part of Wawa Welcome America. Free.
Sunday 7/3
1:00 pm: Historic Philadelphia Block Party in Old City, with food trucks and music. Free.
Monday 7/4
11:00 am: Celebration of Freedom and Independence Day Parade, featuring Tony Award-winning Hamilton actor Leslie Odom Jr. Free.
All Day: Free Museum Day at the National Museum of Jewish American History. Free.
